# Competitor Content Research

Date: 2026-06-12  
Scope: content structure and FAQ inspiration only  
Project: NoordTune.nl main marketing website

## Research Rule

No competitor text was copied into the NoordTune.nl website.

The pages below were reviewed only to understand:

- How customers are introduced to Stage 1 and Stage 2
- What objections and FAQ topics are common
- How diagnostics, dyno/logging and gearbox tuning are explained
- How to structure service pages without thin content
- Where NoordTune.nl should take a more careful and legally precise position

## Sources Reviewed

### TVS Engineering - Chiptuning

URL: `https://tvsengineering.com/en/performance/chiptuning/`

Topics observed:

- Stage-based structure
- Stage 1/Stage 2 expectations
- Engine and gearbox diagnostics mentioned alongside software
- Data analysis and fine-tuning as trust signals

Useful structure ideas:

- Explain Stage 1 and Stage 2 separately.
- Make diagnostics part of the tuning process, not an afterthought.
- Separate "what is included" from "what result can I expect".

NoordTune-original angle:

- Use a more local, direct tone for Assen, Drenthe and Groningen.
- Emphasize realistic margins and vehicle-specific advice over package hype.
- Tie the first step to the NoordTune Power Catalog.

### TVS Engineering - DSG Software

URL: `https://tvsengineering.com/en/performance/dsg-software/`

Topics observed:

- DSG / TCU tuning is explained as shift logic, torque limits and drive modes.
- Different gearbox families and stages have different possibilities.
- Gearbox tuning can be positioned as drivability and torque management, not only speed.

Useful structure ideas:

- Explain why extra engine torque can make TCU/DSG software relevant.
- Mention shift behavior, torque limiters and daily drivability.
- Avoid making every DSG tune sound mandatory.

NoordTune-original angle:

- Explain DSG / TCU in plain Dutch, English and Polish.
- Keep the tone practical: smoother response, better torque handling, still comfortable.

### BR-Performance - Chiptuning / Motor Optimisation

URLs:

- `https://www.br-performance.nl/nl-nl/chiptuning/1-wagens/`
- `https://www.br-performance.be/en-be/chiptuning/1-cars/`

Topics observed:

- Stage 2 is generally explained through supporting hardware and airflow/exhaust efficiency.
- Dyno/results and performance examples are important trust markers.
- Customers expect a clear difference between Stage 1 and Stage 2.

Useful structure ideas:

- Use clear Stage 1 vs Stage 2 copy.
- Treat results as examples, not promises.
- Add "vehicle condition matters" disclaimers near result cards.

NoordTune-original angle:

- Be more careful about exact result claims.
- Connect results pages to the Power Catalog for vehicle-specific indication.

### TC Performance / Polish Tuning Market Examples

URLs:

- `https://www.tc-performance.com/`
- `https://www.tc-performance.eu/en/`

Topics observed:

- Polish and file-service tuning pages often list many services quickly.
- Emissions-related topics are frequently advertised very directly.
- That style can create legal and trust risk for a public main website.

Useful structure ideas:

- Polish users expect terms like Stage 1, Stage 2, DPF, EGR, AdBlue, DSG and TCU.
- Polish pages should explain services clearly, but not sound like a file-service listing.

NoordTune-original angle:

- Use careful legal wording:
  - diagnostyka
  - analiza usterek
  - kontrola systemu
  - doradztwo
  - rozwiązania zgodne z przepisami
  - zależnie od pojazdu, zastosowania i lokalnych przepisów
- Avoid public claims that imply illegal emissions removal is a standard service.

## FAQ Ideas Collected

Useful FAQ topics for NoordTune.nl:

- Is chiptuning safe for my engine?
- What is the difference between Stage 1 and Stage 2?
- Do I need diagnostics before tuning?
- Why are prices indicative?
- Can DSG / TCU tuning help after ECU tuning?
- What should I do with DPF / EGR / AdBlue faults?
- How long does an appointment take?
- Can I check my car before contacting NoordTune?

## NoordTune Content Direction

NoordTune.nl should sound:

- Technical but understandable
- Premium but not exaggerated
- Local to Assen, Drenthe, Groningen and Noord-Nederland
- Honest about vehicle condition and realistic results
- Careful around emissions-system wording
- Strongly connected to the NoordTune Power Catalog as a unique feature

## Confirmation

No text from competitor pages was copied or paraphrased sentence-by-sentence. The final NoordTune.nl copy is original and written for the NoordTune brand, service area and legal positioning.
