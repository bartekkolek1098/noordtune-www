# Internal Linking Plan

Date: `2026-06-14`  
Status: planning for content growth branch

## Principle

Internal links should help the visitor move from education to decision, not create a noisy SEO web. Power Catalog remains the main branded conversion route, while WhatsApp remains the direct advice route.

## Core Hub Pages

- Home: link to Chiptuning, Auto Diagnose, Diensten, Prijzen, Resultaten, Blog, Power Catalog and Contact.
- Chiptuning: link to Stage 1, Stage 2, ECU remap, DSG/TCU, loganalyse, Power Catalog and WhatsApp.
- Auto Diagnose: link to loganalyse, DPF storing, EGR storing, AdBlue storing, diagnostics-before-tuning article and Contact.
- Diensten: link each service card to its strongest supporting page or section.
- Prijzen: link to Power Catalog, WhatsApp, Stage 1, Stage 2 and loganalyse.
- Resultaten: link to Power Catalog, WhatsApp, Stage 1, Stage 2 and future approved customer result pages.
- Blog: link each article to one service page, one related article and one conversion route.

## Customer Results

Customer result detail pages should link to:

- Power Catalog for vehicle-specific indication.
- WhatsApp for advice.
- Relevant service page such as Stage 1, Stage 2, DSG/TCU or loganalyse.
- Relevant brand page only after the brand page is published.

Draft/demo results should not be indexable and should not receive public detail pages.

## Conversion Rules

- Every educational page should include one Power Catalog CTA and one WhatsApp CTA.
- Internal links should be contextual and limited to useful next steps.
- Avoid linking every page to every other page.
- Do not link to draft routes.
- Do not link main website pages to a local `/power` route; use `https://power.noordtune.nl/`.
